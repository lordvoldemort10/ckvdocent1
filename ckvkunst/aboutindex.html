About

Jacob is an art teacher with a passion for teaching and the arts. He has experience teaching at schools, and is now a freelance CKV art teacher.

Teaching approach

Jacob's teaching style is based on the belief that every child has a unique talent that can be nurtured and developed. He encourages students to express themselves through art, and provides them with the skills and knowledge they need to create their own masterpieces.
