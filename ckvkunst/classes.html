Art Teacher's Bio

I am a freelance CKV art teacher with over 10 years of experience. I have worked with various schools and institutions, teaching art to students of all ages. I am passionate about art education and believe that every child should have the opportunity to express themselves creatively. I offer a range of art classes and workshops, covering everything from drawing and painting to art theory and history. My goal is to inspire students to explore their creativity and develop their artistic skills.
